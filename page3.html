What is 3Shape Implant Studio

Consistency and predictability

Implant procedures can be complex. Digital technology can uncomplicate your implant workflow with consistent clinical insight and predictability, based on prosthetic driven implant planning.

3Shape Implant Studio® gives you the tools to deliver the complete or temporary prosthetic solution to the patient at the day of surgery. Depending on whether you want to do planning only, or design surgical guides or provisionals in-house, you can choose Implant Planner or the full Implant Studio package. Expand your offering and make your patients happier. 
Book a demo today

Hardware required

Digital implant treatment planning requires accurate impressions. You can use any DICOM and .STL file in Implant Studio. More about our award-winning scanners. 

The digital implant workflow

From implant planning, surgical guide and prosthetics design to the actual surgery, Implant Studio means a better way to work for you and less chair-time plus more comfort for your patients. Your digital workflow could look as follows:

Scan

Combine any surface and CBCT scan file in Implant Studio to gain complete clinical insight. For consistency and best in class accuracy, we recommend our TRIOS precision intraoral scanner.

Plan

Take advantage of prosthetic driven implant planning while accounting for the clinical situation, including prosthetics, gingiva, bone density and nerve positions. With AI-powered crown proposals and automations, the workflow is easier than ever.

Design

Receive high-quality design proposals for implant crowns or take advantage of powerful editing tools. Additionally, you can fully customize surgical guides in one efficient workflow.

Print

Integrate with in-house printers, or send to a lab or design and production service. No matter your preference, you get fast manufacturing of cost-efficient surgical guides or temporary screw-retained crowns.

Surgery

Offer same-day implant dentistry to your patients when the clinical situation allows for it. Produce the complete treatment package including surgical guide and temporary prosthetic components before surgery. Take advantage of totally data driven and predictable implant planning and placement with the 3Shape TRIOS and 3Shape Implant Studio and X- Guide integration*.

Manufacture

Manufacture the final prosthetic solution in-house or have your lab technician design and produce the full range of prosthetic components.

What you will get

Our implant planning software is based on three principles: intended final restoration, available bone and the overall clinical situation. The solution increases treatment predictability and helps deliver a better outcome.

Treat any implant case

Implat Studio is a go-to solution for all implant cases. You can start with single unit cases and then progress to multiple implant cases or even fully edentulous cases. For invasive and predictable surgery design tooth-, mucousa- or bone-supported guides.

A guided workflow

Implant Studio has an easy to follow, step-by-step workflow. To decrease the learning curve we have introduced a hints & notification system that helps you in design steps by offering tips & tricks or reminding you how to solve design related issues.

Offer same-day implant dentistry

Diagnostic tools help you decide if cases are appropriate for immediate loading. You can virtually remove teeth and then design a surgical guide and the temporary prosthetics for same-day placement.

Get started

If you want to start with planning only, 3Shape Implant Planner lets you plan any implant treatment. Plan placement with consistent digital precision and efficiency, then share your cases with your preferred lab for the design and production of surgical guides and provisional or final restorations. Implant Planner is a subset of Implant Studio and a great opportunity to start your journey into digital implantology within the 3Shape ecosystem - at a low investment.
